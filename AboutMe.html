About Me

I'm a 20 year old musician currently studying at the University of the Arts in Utrecht. I've always been drawn to all kinds of art; drawing, writing, telling stories, creating games and (of course) composing. Among those passions music ended up taking the foreground and since I was a teen I've been using that drive to create pieces meant to conjure all sorts of emotions.

At this point I'm studying to not just create my art as an individual, but to make it auxiliary to the products of other artists, especially in the field of video games. School has already provided some amazing opportunities in this respect, but the next steps I hope to take are to participate in Game Jams and to intern at a video game company.
